Vision

Our Vision is to foster the vibrant, collaborative ecosystem at the Texas Medical Center, leveraging its unparalleled talent, diverse data resources, and entrepreneurial energy to translate advanced AI innovations into practical solutions for real-world biomedical and healthcare challenges.

By uniting researchers, clinicians, and industry leaders, we aim to revolutionize healthcare delivery, improve outcomes, and catalyze meaningful impact for everyone, throughout the entire human lifespan.

Our commitment ensures that AI technologies in healthcare generate tangible benefits for individuals, healthcare systems, and communities worldwide.

Call for Participation – TMC AI Summit 2026

We are excited to announce the Call for Participation for the TMC AI Summit 2026, co-hosted with Texas Children’s Hospital, taking place February 18–20, 2026 at the TCH Neurological Research Institute (NRI) in Houston, Texas.

This event is dedicated to foster the vibrant, collaborative ecosystem at the Texas Medical Center, leveraging its unparalleled talent, diverse data resources, and entrepreneurial energy to translate advanced AI innovations into practical solutions for real-world biomedical and healthcare challenges.

By uniting researchers, clinicians, and industry leaders, we aim to revolutionize healthcare delivery, improve outcomes, and catalyze meaningful impact for everyone, throughout the entire human lifespan.

Our commitment ensures that AI technologies in healthcare generate tangible benefits for individuals, healthcare systems, and communities worldwide.

Join us to engage with world-class researchers, clinicians, and industry leaders building the future of AI-driven healthcare.

Program Tracks:

Foundation Track: Explore innovations in data science, AI, and informatics that are reshaping healthcare by empowering data-driven discovery, fostering precision medicine, and optimizing healthcare outcomes.

Opportunity Track: Showcase transformative AI applications in biomedical discovery and healthcare, including diagnostic tools, imaging analysis, drug development, and more.

Impact Track: Discuss strategies for AI translation and stories of successful implementation with meaningful impact.

Submission Opportunities
