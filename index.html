Mission

Our mission is to foster a vibrant, collaborative ecosystem at the Texas Medical Center, leveraging its unparalleled talent, diverse data resources, and entrepreneurial energy to translate advanced AI innovations into practical solutions for real-world biomedical and healthcare challenges. By uniting researchers, clinicians, and industry leaders, we aim to revolutionize healthcare delivery, improve outcomes, and catalyze meaningful, global impact. Our commitment ensures that AI technologies in healthcare generate tangible benefits for individuals, healthcare systems, and communities worldwide.

Call for Participation

We are thrilled to announce the Call for Participation for the Texas Medical Center (TMC) AI Summit 2025, a flagship event hosted by TMC institutions. Join us on February 20-21, 2025, at TMC Helix Park in Houston, TX!

This summit is dedicated to cultivating a vibrant, collaborative ecosystem at the Texas Medical Center by harnessing its world-class talent, diverse data resources, and entrepreneurial spirit. Our goal is to translate cutting-edge AI innovations into actionable solutions for real-world biomedical and healthcare challenges. By bringing together researchers, clinicians, and industry leaders, we aim to revolutionize healthcare delivery, enhance outcomes, and drive impactful, global change.

Program Tracks

    Foundation Track:

    • Explore innovations in data science, AI, and informatics that are reshaping healthcare.
    • Submissions should highlight advanced methodologies in data-driven discovery, precision medicine, and informatics frameworks for optimizing outcomes.

    Opportunity Track:

    • Showcase transformative AI applications in biomedical discovery and healthcare, including diagnostic tools, imaging analysis, drug development, and more.

    Impact Track:

    • Discuss challenges and strategies for AI integration, including regulatory and ethical considerations, safety and fairness, educational approaches, and clinical deployment.

Please join us in shaping the future of biomedicine and healthcare with AI. This is a prime opportunity to share your findings, interact with experts, and contribute to an ever-evolving field.

Submissions

Poster and Oral Abstract Submission


  • Abstract Length: 500 words maximum
  • Content: Provide a concise summary of your research, innovation, or application relevant to the selected track (Foundation, Applied, or Implementation).

Workshop and Tutorial Submission


  • Speaker Information: List all speakers in the order they should appear in the program, including their affiliations.
  • Summary: Include a 500-word maximum description detailing the takeaway skills, knowledge, or material participants will acquire.
    • Note: This summary will also be used for the program description.

Student Research Showcase Submission

Submissions should include the following sections:


  • Aims and Objectives: Clearly state the primary goals of your project.
  • Justification for the Research Topic: Explain the motivation and significance of your research.
  • Research Questions: List the key research questions you aim to address.
  • Research Methodology:
    • Describe your planned or ongoing research methods.
    • If undecided, list the requirements your methodology should fulfill.
  • Research Results to Date:
    • While results are not required, please include any preliminary findings if available.
  • References: Include any citations relevant to your research.
